Privacy Policy — Perfect Stack

Effective date: March 4, 2026

KWNG ("we", "us", or "our") built Perfect Stack as a free game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Perfect Stack collects minimal data to provide core game functionality:

  • Nickname: A display name you choose, stored locally and on our leaderboard server.
  • Best Score: Your highest score, stored locally and on our leaderboard server.
  • Device Identifier: A randomly generated ID (not your real device ID) used to associate your nickname and score on the leaderboard. This ID is created on first launch and stored locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No real names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No contacts or photos
  • No payment or financial information
  • No advertising identifiers

Data Storage

Game preferences (theme, design, background, coins, music settings) are stored locally on your device only. Nicknames and best scores are stored on Google Firebase Firestore to power the global leaderboard.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Google Firebase Firestore: To store leaderboard data (nickname, best score, device-generated ID). See Firebase Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Perfect Stack does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only user-provided data is a self-chosen nickname. No real identity information is required or collected.

Data Deletion

You can reset your data at any time by using the "Change Nickname" option in the game settings, which clears your local data. To request deletion of your leaderboard data, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
